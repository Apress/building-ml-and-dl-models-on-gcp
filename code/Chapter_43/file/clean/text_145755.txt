Please bear these points in mind while editing Wikipedia
 Respect intellectual property rights  do not copy and paste text or images directly from other websites
 Maintain a neutral point of view when editing articles  this is possibly the most important Wikipedia policy
 If you are testing please use the Sandbox to do so 
 Do not add troublesome content to any article such as copyrighted text advertising or promotional messages and text that is not related to an articles subject Adding such information or otherwise editing articles maliciously is considered vandalism doing so will result your account being blocked from editing
