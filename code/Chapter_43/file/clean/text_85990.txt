Terrorist threat 
See 2000 Amarnath pilgrimage massacre

The cave is situated at a place where Kashmiri extremists have been very active recently and thus the pilgrimage is under the constant threat of terrorist attack However many pilgrims still continue to brave the attacks and visit the shrine each year There were a number of terrorist attacks in the recent past and dozens of pilgrims die in them every year
