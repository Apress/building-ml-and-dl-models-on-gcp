On September 23 2008 at Kauhajoki in Finland a 22 year old culinary student named Matti Saari shot and killed ten students before killing himself  
The official report on the shooting has been released by the Finnish Ministry and on page 58 of that report PDF file it states that Matti Saari was taking an SSRI medicinal product and also a benzodiazepine
